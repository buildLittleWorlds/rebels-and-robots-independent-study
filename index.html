Rebels and Robots: A Character Analysis System

Welcome

This project presents the "Rebels and Robots" character analysis system, designed to help students understand how characters interact with social and systemic forces, especially within the context of modern literature. The project was designed to make it possible to give a motivated undergraduate a meaningful and rigorous independent study experience.

The goal is to facilitate rigorous analysis while emphasizing objectivity, self-directed learning, and the ability to apply critical thinking to various texts.
